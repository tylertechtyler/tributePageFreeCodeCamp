Here are some of Dr King's many accomplishments

  1. He led the Montgomery Bus boycott in 1955
  2. He led the Birmingham campaign
  3. He was the first president of the Southern Christian Leadership Conference
  4. He organized the great march on Washington in 1963
  5. He gave many speeches that inspired and motivated the civil rights movement
  6. He was Time Magazine's Man of the Year in 1963
  7. He bacame the youngest recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize in 1964
  8. He became reknowned world wide as the symbolic leader for African Americans
